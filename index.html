About Me

My name is Wendy and I would like to tell you a little bit about my LEGO collection journey. It all started when my parents bought some sets for me that I absolutely fell in love with. When it really kicked off was back in December of 2014. I bought my very first LEGO Star Wars set with my own money. It was an AT-AT. Still have it sealed to this day. I now have over 300 LEGO sets. I would like for you to be apart of my building journey. Here's my Linkedin if you want to follow my journey.

The Journey to Becoming a Software Developer

Thanks to Amazon's Career Choice program, I'm currently enrolled in the Software Development course lead by Correlation One. I always knew I wanted a career that had something to do with computers but I was a bit scared to take a chance. For the longest time I just never took that chance becuase I was scared of failure but this year I set my nerves aside and took that chance. I'm glad I did take that leap of faith because now I can't wait to see where this new career path will take me.
